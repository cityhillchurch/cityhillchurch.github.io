Welcome to our Ministries page! At City Hill we have a variety of ministries and serving opportunities that you can join.

Life Groups

At City Hill we value getting together in smaller settings to share life and to study God’s Word. We usually share a dinner and then go into an in-depth study of God’s Word, while sharing joys and burdens with one another.It’s never to late to join one so we hope you can make it out!

Please contact cityhillchurch.org if you need more information or if you wanted to join one!

Mercy/Outreach

Every month or so City Hill hosts an outreach or mercy event in the city of Chicago. We seek opportunities to help those who are in need, while sharing and proclaiming the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Please contact us if you need more information!

Prayer

Our corporate time of prayer is once a month (3rd Saturdays) at one of our member’s apartments. We also have informal get-togethers for prayer during the week. Please keep up-to-date with meeting information on our Home/Announcements page.
